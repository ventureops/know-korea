# FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions
> Can't find what you're looking for? [Ask a question →](/community/new)

## 🇰🇷 About This Site
**What is Know Korea?** Know Korea is a practical information hub for foreigners living in, moving to, or curious about Korea. Every guide is written around a single real-life question — no fluff, just what you actually need to know.
**Who writes the content?** All guides and articles are written and reviewed by the site admin, based on real experience living and working in Korea. We don't publish AI-generated content without human review.
**Is this official information from the Korean government?** No. We are an independent platform. While we do our best to keep information accurate and up to date, this site is not affiliated with any government body. For legal, visa, or immigration matters, always verify with official sources (e.g. HiKorea, Ministry of Justice).
**How often is content updated?** We update guides when laws, policies, or procedures change. Each article shows the last updated date at the top.

## 🔍 Finding Information
**How do I find what I need?** Three ways:
1. Use the **search bar** at the top — just type a keyword
2. Browse **categories** in the left sidebar
3. Check the **Start Here** guide if you're brand new to Korea
**I searched but couldn't find my answer. What now?** Post your question in **Community**. The community or admin will answer, and your question may become a future guide.
**Can I request a topic or guide?** Yes — use Community and tag it as a content request. We prioritize topics that come up repeatedly.

## 💬 Community
**What's the difference between Comments and Community?**
- **Comments** are reactions to a specific article — agree, add context, share your experience
- **Community** is for asking new questions that aren't covered in existing content
**Do I need an account to read Community posts?** No. Anyone can read Community posts. But to post a question or reply, you need to be logged in.
**How do I write a good question?**
- Be specific: "How do I open a bank account at Kakao Bank as a foreigner on a D-10 visa?" is better than "How do I open a bank account?"
- Choose the right category
- Check if a similar question already exists before posting
**Can I answer other people's questions?** Yes — any logged-in member can reply to Community posts.
**Will the admin always answer?** We do our best, but response time varies. Community members often answer faster for common questions.

## 👤 Account & Profile
**How do I create an account?** Click **Login** in the top navigation and choose Google or Apple sign-in. No password needed — we use your existing account securely.
**Do you store my password?** No. We never see or store your password. Login is handled entirely by Google or Apple.
**How do I change my nickname or profile photo?** Go to your **Profile** page (click your avatar in the top right) and edit from there.
**Can I use a pseudonym?** Yes. Your display nickname doesn't have to match your real name.
**How do I delete my account?** Go to Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account. Your posts will remain but be shown as "Deleted User" to preserve conversation context.

## ✅ Read Tracking
**What is the "Read" button?** It's a way to track which guides you've completed — similar to how Skool works. Click the button on any article to mark it as read. It turns green when marked.
**Where can I see my reading progress?** In the left sidebar — when you open a category, each content card shows your read/unread status at a glance.
**Do I need to be logged in?** Yes. Read tracking is tied to your account so it syncs across devices.

## ☕ Ko-fi Support
**What is the Ko-fi button?** It's an optional way to support the site if a guide helped you. There's no subscription, no paywall — all content is free. Ko-fi is just a tip jar.
**Is my payment secure?** Ko-fi transactions are handled entirely by Ko-fi's platform. We never see your payment details. See their [privacy policy](https://ko-fi.com/knowkorea).

## 🔒 Privacy & Safety
**What data do you collect?** Only what's needed to run the site: your email (for login), nickname, profile photo, and activity like comments and Community posts. We don't sell data. Ever. See our full [Privacy Policy](/legal).
**Is my email visible to other users?** No. Only your nickname and profile photo are public.
**How do I report a comment or post?** Use the report button on any comment or Community post. Or email us directly at **poisian@gmail.com**.
